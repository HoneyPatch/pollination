Adjustable Parameters:
1) Height
2) Taper
3) South Glazing Ratio
4) South Shading Depth
5) East Shading Depth
6) West Shading Depth
7) Heating Setpoint
8) Cooling Setpoint

Output Variables:
1) Heatingload
2) Cooling load
3) Lighting
4) Equipment
5) Comfortable Occupied hours
6) Hot Occupied hours
7) Cold Occupied hours

Fittness = Heatingload + Cooling load + Lighting + Equipment + Hot Occupied hours + Cold Occupied hours
